FAQs

Questions, Answered

Most adults should have a full eye test every 1–2 years, or sooner if you notice changes in your vision, headaches, or eye strain. People with diabetes, high blood pressure, or a family history of glaucoma should test more often.

Walk-ins are welcome, but booking ahead means less waiting. You can book on this site or send us a quick WhatsApp message and we'll reserve a slot for you.
Glaucoma screening checks the pressure inside your eye (and other signs) to detect glaucoma early — before it damages your sight. It's quick and painless. We recommend it especially if you're over 40 or have a family history of glaucoma.
Absolutely. We offer gentle, child-friendly eye checks. Catching vision problems early helps children do better in school and sport. You can bring the whole family in one visit.
